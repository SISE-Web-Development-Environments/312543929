    Greta Thunberg

    Greta Thunberg is an environmental activist who has gained international recognition for promoting the view that humanity is facing an existential crisis arising from climate change.
    She was born on 3 January 2003 in Stockholm, Sweden.
    Thunberg is well known for her youth and her straightfoward speaking manner in which she criticizes world leaders for their failure to take sufficient action to address the climate crisis.
    At age 15, she started spending her school days outside the Swedish parliament to call for stronger action on climate change by holding up a sign reading "School Strike".
    Thunberg said her teachers were divided in their views about her missing class to make her point.
    Her influence on the world stage has been described by The Guardian and other newspapers as the "Greta effect".
